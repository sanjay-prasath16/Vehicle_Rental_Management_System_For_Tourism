ENGINE

The Yamaha MT-09 is powered by a potent 847cc inline three-cylinder engine, known for its impressive torque and exhilarating performance. This liquid-cooled, DOHC engine features a crossplane crankshaft design, enhancing throttle response and delivering a smooth, linear power curve. With an output of around 115 horsepower, it offers strong acceleration and a thrilling ride. The engine is equipped with advanced fuel injection and ride-by-wire technology, ensuring precise control and efficiency. Additionally, the MT-09's engine benefits from Yamaha's innovative CP3 (Crossplane Concept) technology, which provides a unique and engaging riding experience, characterized by its distinctive exhaust note and robust mid-range power.
